About Our College

Established with a vision to provide quality education to all sections of society

Welcome to Our Prestigious Institution

Government Arts & Science College, Thiruvvidaimaruthur is a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development. Established to empower rural and urban youth, our college is affiliated with Bharathidasan University and offers a wide range of undergraduate programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce.

  • Accredited institution recognized for quality education
  • Inclusive campus welcoming students from all backgrounds
  • Active student life with clubs, sports, and cultural events
  • Career guidance and placement cell for employability
  • Community service through NSS, NCC, and outreach programs

Our Motto: Education for Empowerment & Social Transformation

Our Vision: To foster academic excellence and create responsible citizens

Our Mission: Providing affordable, inclusive, and quality education
